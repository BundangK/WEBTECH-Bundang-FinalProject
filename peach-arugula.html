Grilled Pork Chops with Arugula and Peaches

Savor the delicious combination of our Grilled Pork Chops with Arugula and Peaches for a delightful meal! Each juicy pork chop is perfectly grilled, delivering a smoky flavor that pairs beautifully with the sweetness of fresh, ripe peaches. The peppery arugula adds a refreshing crunch, making this dish not only flavorful but also vibrant and satisfying. This meal is a fantastic source of protein and essential nutrients, perfect for lunch or dinner. Impress your family and friends with this elegant yet simple dish that brings together sweet and savory in every bite!

Recipe Credits

Recipe by Mary Claire Britton

Video by Tatyana's Everyday Food

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 clove garlic, finely chopped
  • 1 ½ teaspoons kosher salt, divided
  • 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper, divided
  • 6 tablespoons olive oil, divided, plus more for grill grates
  • 4 ½-inch-thick bone-in pork chops (about 2 lb.)
  • 4 cups arugula (about 4 oz.)
  • 1 cup loosely packed fresh flat-leaf parsley leaves, torn
  • 4 ripe peaches, nectarines, or apricots, pitted and sliced

Directions

  1. Preheat grill to high (450°F to 500°F) and lightly oil grates.
  2. Whisk vinegar, honey, garlic, ½ teaspoon each salt and pepper, and 5 tablespoons oil.
  3. Pat pork chops dry and brush with remaining 1 tablespoon oil. Season both sides with remaining 1 teaspoon salt and teaspoon pepper.
  4. Grill, uncovered, flipping once, until a meat thermometer inserted in thickest portion registers 145°F, 3 to 4 minutes per side. Let rest for 10 minutes.
  5. Meanwhile, toss arugula with parsley and peaches. Serve with pork, drizzled with vinaigrette.
