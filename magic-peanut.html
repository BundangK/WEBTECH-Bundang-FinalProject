Peanut Butter Yogurt Cup with Magic Shell Topping

Treat yourself to our indulgent Peanut Butter Yogurt Cup with Magic Shell Topping! Creamy peanut butter yogurt forms the base of this delicious dessert, delivering a rich and nutty flavor. Topped with a delightful magic shell that hardens on contact, it adds a fun crunch to each bite. This sweet treat is not only satisfying but also packs a protein punch. Perfect for a quick snack or a guilt-free dessert, it’s a delightful way to satisfy your cravings!

Ingredients

  • 1 (5.3-oz.) container low-fat vanilla strained (Greek-style) yogurt
  • 2 teaspoons natural peanut butter
  • 1 tablespoon dark chocolate chips
  • 1/2 teaspoon coconut oil
  • Chopped unsalted peanuts for garnish (optional)
  • Flaky sea salt for garnish (optional)

Directions

  1. Mix yogurt and peanut butter together in the yogurt container or a small bowl.
  2. Combine chocolate chips and coconut oil in a small microwave-safe bowl. Microwave on High in 15-second intervals, stirring after each, until the chocolate is melted and smooth (about 45 to 60 seconds total).
  3. Pour the melted chocolate over the yogurt mixture and spread evenly to cover. Garnish with peanuts and/or salt, if desired.
  4. Freeze for 10 minutes or refrigerate for 30 minutes, until the chocolate is set.
  5. To make ahead, refrigerate for up to 3 days.
