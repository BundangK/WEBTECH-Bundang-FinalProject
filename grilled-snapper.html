Grilled Red Snapper

Experience the vibrant flavors of Grilled Red Snapper, a dish that's sure to impress! This tender fish is expertly grilled to perfection, creating a beautifully charred exterior while maintaining a flaky, moist interior. Enhanced with a sprinkle of fresh herbs and a squeeze of zesty lemon, each bite is a taste of the ocean's bounty. Served alongside a colorful medley of grilled vegetables or a light salad, this meal is both nutritious and satisfying. Perfect for a summer dinner or a special occasion, Grilled Red Snapper is a delicious way to enjoy a healthy, flavorful feast!

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon ground pepper
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 4 (5 ounce) boneless, skinless red snapper fillets
  • 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
  • Lemon wedges

Directions

  1. Preheat grill to medium-high (400˚F-450˚F).
  2. Combine paprika, pepper, onion powder, garlic powder, oregano, thyme, salt, and cayenne in a small bowl.
  3. Brush fish all over with oil; sprinkle the seasoning evenly over both sides of the fish, pressing to adhere.
  4. Oil the grill grates. Grill the fish, covered, until it flakes easily and a thermometer inserted in the thickest part registers 145˚F, about 3 to 5 minutes per side.
  5. Serve with lemon wedges.
