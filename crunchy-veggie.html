Crunchy Veggie Wrap

Snack time just got better with our Crunchy Veggie Wrap! This tasty wrap is filled with a colorful mix of fresh vegetables that provide a satisfying crunch in every bite. With a creamy spread to tie it all together, it’s a delightful and nutritious snack that’s perfect for any time of day. Packed with vitamins and fiber, it’s not just delicious but also a healthy choice. Quick to make and easy to grab, this wrap is the ideal snack for your busy lifestyle!

Ingredients

  • ½ of a medium avocado, seeded and peeled
  • 1 teaspoon lime juice
  • 2 8-inch light flatbread spinach wraps, or whole wheat low-carb tortillas
  • 1 small cucumber, cut into thin bite-size strips
  • 1 medium carrot, cut into thin bite-size strips
  • ½ of a small red sweet pepper, cut into thin bite-size strips
  • 2 tablespoons crumbled feta cheese

Directions

  1. In a small bowl, mash together avocado and lime juice. Divide the avocado mixture between tortillas, spreading evenly.
  2. Arrange cucumber, carrot, and sweet pepper strips on top of the avocado mixture, leaving about 1 inch of space at the ends of each tortilla.
  3. Sprinkle with feta cheese; roll up each tortilla into a spiral. Serve immediately or cover and chill for up to 4 hours. Cut in half diagonally before serving.
